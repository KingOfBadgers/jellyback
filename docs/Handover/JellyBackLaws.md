JELLYBACK LAW 1
Never build intelligence into Stage 3.

Stage 3 is presentation.

Stage 2 resolves assets.

Stage 3 only offers choices.
JELLYBACK LAW 2
No variant generator may render.

Generators only expose valid choices.
JELLYBACK LAW 3
Renderer receives prebuilt instructions.

Renderer does not think.


JELLYBACK LAW 4
BACKGROUND and BACKDROPS are not the same concept.

BACKGROUND

= the single canonical composition background.

Exactly one.

Chosen in Stage 2.

Inherited by Stage 3.

BACKDROPS

= raw artwork assets inherited from Jellyfin.

Zero to many.

Used for collage and composition variants.

Stage 3 may never substitute BACKDROPS for BACKGROUND.

JELLYBACK LAW 5

The Stage 3 canvas is always 1000x1500.

The composition canvas may never scale.

Only the viewport wrapper may scale for display purposes.

Composition coordinates must remain absolute and deterministic.
